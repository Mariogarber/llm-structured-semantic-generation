Set up a backend Envoy YAML with 2 services. 
1st service relies on a Dockerfile in parent dir, verifies 2nd service health on launch. 
1st service maps ports dynamically (8002 for backend, 8003 for stats), links to 10000 and 8001 in container.
2nd service builds using context in python dir one level up. Reflects changes to local service.py by mapping to /code/service.py in container.
