修改 DaemonSet 配置以容忍在主节点和控制平面节点上运行。
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: nginx-daemon
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      name: nginx-daemon
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        name: nginx-daemon
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: nginx
        image: nginx
```
