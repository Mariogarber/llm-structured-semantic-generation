你能修复这个 k8s ClusterRole manifest 中的错误吗？将 "get" 用作默认的 verbs。
```
apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
kind: ClusterRole
metadata:
  name: buggy-clusterrole
rules:
  - apiGroups: [""]
    resources: ["pods"]
    verbs: ["got", "list", "watch"]
  - apiGroups: [""]
    resources: ["services"]
  - apiGroups: ["apps"]
    resources: ["deployments"]
    verbs: ["get", "list"]
  - apiGroups: ["batch"]
    resources: ["jobs"]
    verbs: ["create", "delete", "delete"]
```
