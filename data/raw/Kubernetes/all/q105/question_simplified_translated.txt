修改给定的 K8S Job 配置以模拟软件错误。如果主容器以状态码 42 退出，则 Job 应该失败。Kubernetes 在放弃之前应该重试该任务最多 3 次。
```
apiVersion: batch/v1
kind: Job
metadata:
  name: failjob
spec:
  completions: 4
  parallelism: 1
  template:
    spec:
      restartPolicy: Never
      containers:
      - name: main
        image: alpine
        command:
        - "sh"
        - "-c"
        - "sleep 5"
```
