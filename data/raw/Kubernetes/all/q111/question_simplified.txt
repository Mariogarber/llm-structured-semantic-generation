Configure a Kubernetes job manifest (YAML) to create a job with kube-job- as a prefix. Label the job with "jobgroup: jexample" and use the following spec:
```
spec:
  template:
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: my-container
        image: busybox
        command: ["echo", "Hello from the job"]
      restartPolicy: OnFailure
```
