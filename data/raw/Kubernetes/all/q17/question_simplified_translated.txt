为名为hostalias-daemonset的DaemonSet指定一个主机别名。使用IP地址"10.1.2.3"，并将主机名设置为"myapp.local"。
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: hostalias-daemonset
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: hostalias-daemonset
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: hostalias-daemonset
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: busybox
        image: busybox
        command: ["sleep", "3600"]
```
