调试给定的 YAML 并修改它，使其只在失败时重新启动。以下是修正后的 YAML：
```
apiVersion: batch/v1
kind: Job
metadata:
  name: hello
spec:
  template:
    metadata:
      name: hello
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: hello
        image: python:latest
        command: ["python",  "-c", "print('Hello world!')"]
      restartPolicy: Always
```
