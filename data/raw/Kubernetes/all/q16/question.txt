Given the YAML describing a DaemonSet for `fluentd-elasticsearch`, Add the necessary `tolerations` to allow the DaemonSet to be 
scheduled on master nodes. Ensure that the specific "key" used to identify master nodes is `node-role.kubernetes.io/master`.
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: fluentd-elasticsearch
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      name: fluentd-elasticsearch
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        name: fluentd-elasticsearch
    spec:
      containers:
        - image: quay.io/fluentd_elasticsearch/fluentd:v2.5.2
          name: fluentd-elasticsearch
```
