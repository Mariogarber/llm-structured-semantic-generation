将 `tolerations` 与键 `node-role.kubernetes.io/master` 添加到 `fluentd-elasticsearch` 的 `DaemonSet` YAML 中，以允许在主节点上进行调度。
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: fluentd-elasticsearch
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      name: fluentd-elasticsearch
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        name: fluentd-elasticsearch
    spec:
      containers:
        - image: quay.io/fluentd_elasticsearch/fluentd:v2.5.2
          name: fluentd-elasticsearch
```
