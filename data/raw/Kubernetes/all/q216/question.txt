You have an existing Kubernetes ClusterRole. The role has the following YAML configuration:
```
apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
kind: ClusterRole
metadata:
  name: existing-clusterrole
rules:
  - apiGroups: [""]
    resources: ["pods", "services"]
    verbs: ["get", "list", "watch"]
  - apiGroups: ["apps"]
    resources: ["deployments"]
    verbs: ["get", "list"]
  - apiGroups: ["batch"]
    resources: ["jobs"]
    verbs: ["create", "delete"]
```
Your task is to modify it in the following ways: (a) Change the verbs for "services" to only "get"; (b) Remove permissions for "jobs" in the "batch" apiGroup; (c) Add permissions to "watch" configmaps in the "" apiGroup.
