修改现有的 K8s ClusterRole YAML 如下：(a) 将 "services" 动词限制为 "get"；(b) 在 "batch" apiGroup 中删除 "jobs" 权限；(c) 在 "" apiGroup 中添加 "watch" 权限，用于 configmaps。给定：
```
apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
kind: ClusterRole
metadata:
  name: existing-clusterrole
rules:
  - apiGroups: [""]
    resources: ["pods", "services"]
    verbs: ["get", "list", "watch"]
  - apiGroups: ["apps"]
    resources: ["deployments"]
    verbs: ["get", "list"]
  - apiGroups: ["batch"]
    resources: ["jobs"]
    verbs: ["create", "delete"]
```
