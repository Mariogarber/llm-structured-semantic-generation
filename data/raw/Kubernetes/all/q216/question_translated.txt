你有一个现有的 Kubernetes ClusterRole。该角色具有以下 YAML 配置：
```
apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
kind: ClusterRole
metadata:
  name: existing-clusterrole
rules:
  - apiGroups: [""]
    resources: ["pods", "services"]
    verbs: ["get", "list", "watch"]
  - apiGroups: ["apps"]
    resources: ["deployments"]
    verbs: ["get", "list"]
  - apiGroups: ["batch"]
    resources: ["jobs"]
    verbs: ["create", "delete"]
```
你的任务是按以下方式修改它：(a) 将 "services" 的权限更改为仅 "get"；(b) 删除 "batch" apiGroup 中 "jobs" 的权限；(c) 添加对 "" apiGroup 中 "watch" configmaps 的权限。
