提供一个简短的 K8s YAML，用于修复在尝试获取或描述 "secret-sa-sample" 密钥时出现 "secrets "secret-sa-sample" not found" 问题。
```
apiVersion: v1
kind: Secret
metadata:
  name: secret-sa-sample
  annotations:
    kubernetes.io/service-account.name: "sa-name"
type: kubernetes.io/service-account-token
data:
  extra: YmFyCg==     
```
