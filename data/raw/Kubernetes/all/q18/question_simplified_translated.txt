将给定的 DaemonSet YAML 添加一个 nodeSelector，以确保 Pod 仅在标记为 "disktype=ssd" 的节点上运行。
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: nodeselector-daemonset
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: nodeselector-daemonset
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: nodeselector-daemonset
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: busybox
        image: busybox
        command: ["sleep", "3600"]
```
