将 PersistentVolume 限制为具有区域 "topology.kubernetes.io/zone" 为 `us-central1-a` 和 `us-central1-b` 的节点。给定：
```
apiVersion: v1
kind: PersistentVolume
metadata:
  name: test-volume
spec:
  capacity:
    storage: 50Gi
  storageClassName: storage-test-volume
  accessModes:
    - ReadWriteOnce
  gcePersistentDisk:
    pdName: my-data-disk
    fsType: ext4
```
