Add two containers to the DaemonSet:
- Container 1: busybox image, continuously echo 'Container 1 Running' every 10 seconds.
- Container 2: busybox image, continuously echo 'Container 2 Running' every 15 seconds.
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: ds-multi-container
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      name: ds-multi-container
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        name: ds-multi-container
```
