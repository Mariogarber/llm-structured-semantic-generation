修改给定的 K8s YAML，在部署成功初始化后运行命令 "mkdir tt"。提供完整修改后的 YAML 文件。
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: Deployment
metadata:
  name: nginx-test
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: nginx
  replicas: 2
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: nginx
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: nginx
        image: nginx:1.16.1
        ports:
        - containerPort: 80
```
