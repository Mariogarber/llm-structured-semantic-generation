给 DaemonSet 添加两个容器：“container-main” 使用 busybox 镜像和命令 "while true; do echo 'Main container'; sleep 10; done"，以及 “container-sidecar” 使用 busybox 镜像和命令 "while true; do ps aux; sleep 15; done"。设置适当的字段为 true，以确保容器共享相同的进程命名空间。
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: ds-share-process-ns
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      name: ds-share-process-ns
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        name: ds-share-process-ns
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: container-main
        image: busybox
        command: [ "sh", "-c", "while true; do echo 'Main container'; sleep 10; done" ]
      - name: container-sidecar
        image: busybox
        command: [ "sh", "-c", "while true; do ps aux; sleep 15; done" ]
```
