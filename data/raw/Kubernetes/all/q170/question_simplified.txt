Add liveness probe to Pod, checking /tmp/healthy every 5s. First probe after 5s.
```
apiVersion: v1
kind: Pod
metadata:
  labels:
    test: liveness
  name: liveness-exec
spec:
  containers:
  - name: liveness
    image: registry.k8s.io/busybox
    args:
    - /bin/sh
    - -c
    - touch /tmp/healthy; sleep 20; rm -f /tmp/healthy; sleep 600
```
