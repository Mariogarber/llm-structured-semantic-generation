修改现有的 YAML 资源以通过 HPA 成功扩展前端 ReplicaSet。
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: ReplicaSet
metadata:
  name: frontend
spec:
  replicas: 3
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: frontend-app
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: frontend-app
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: frontend-container
        image: nginx:latest
---
apiVersion: autoscaling/v1
kind: HorizontalPodAutoscaler
metadata:
  name: frontend-scaler
spec:
  scaleTargetRef:
    kind: ReplicaSet
    name: frontend
  minReplicas: 3
  maxReplicas: 10
  targetCPUUtilizationPercentage: 50
```
