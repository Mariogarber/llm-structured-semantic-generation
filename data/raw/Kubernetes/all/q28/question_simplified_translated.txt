在给定的 YAML 中为 DaemonSet 容器设置一个活跃探针。探针应该检查 /tmp/healthy 文件的存在，从容器启动后的 5 秒开始，每 5 秒重复一次。
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: ds-liveness-probe
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      name: ds-liveness-probe
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        name: ds-liveness-probe
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: ds-liveness-container
        image: busybox
        args:
        - /bin/sh
        - -c
        - touch /tmp/healthy; sleep 5; rm -f /tmp/healthy; sleep 600
```
