定义 "nginx-container-limits" 容器在 ReplicaSet YAML 中的资源限制和请求。将请求设置为 "64Mi" 内存和 "250m" CPU，将限制设置为 "128Mi" 内存和 "500m" CPU。
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: ReplicaSet
metadata:
  name: nginx-replicaset-limits
spec:
  replicas: 2
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: nginx-limits
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: nginx-limits
```
