将终止优雅期设置为60秒，并在以下的DaemonSet YAML中使用服务账户'default'。
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: ds-grace-serviceaccount
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      name: ds-grace-serviceaccount
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        name: ds-grace-serviceaccount
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: nginx
        image: nginx:latest
```
