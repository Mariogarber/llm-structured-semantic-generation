修改现有的 DaemonSet，通过使用 busybox 镜像添加 Init 容器。Init 容器创建 /var/shared-data/hello.txt 文件，内容为 "Hello from Init"。
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: my-ds
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      name: my-ds
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        name: my-ds
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: nginx
        image: nginx
        volumeMounts:
        - name: shared-data
          mountPath: /var/shared-data
      volumes:
      - name: shared-data
        emptyDir: {}
```
