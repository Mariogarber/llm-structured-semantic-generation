修改现有的 DaemonSet 为：
- 为容器设置 CPU 限制为 500m，内存限制为 100Mi。
- 在容器中将共享数据卷挂载到 /var/shared-data。
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: my-ds
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      name: my-ds
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        name: my-ds
    spec:
      nodeSelector:
        kubernetes.io/os: linux
      containers:
      - name: nginx
        image: nginx
        livenessProbe:
          httpGet:
            path: /
            port: 80
          initialDelaySeconds: 5
          timeoutSeconds: 1
        readinessProbe:
          httpGet:
            path: /
            port: 80
          initialDelaySeconds: 5
          timeoutSeconds: 1
```
