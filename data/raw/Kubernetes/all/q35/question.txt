Given the Kubernetes structure below, I'd like you to incorporate the utilization of a Service Account for the DaemonSet. Here are the specifics I need:
1. Create a Service Account with a unique identifier.
2. Use the above Service Account in a DaemonSet definition, the DaemonSet should have a container that continuously prints a specific message every 10 seconds.
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: ds-service-account-set
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      name: ds-service-account-set
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        name: ds-service-account-set
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: ds-service-account-container
        image: busybox
        command: [ "sh", "-c", "while true; do echo 'Running with service account'; sleep 10; done" ]
```
