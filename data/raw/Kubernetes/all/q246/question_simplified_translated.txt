创建一个 Kubernetes 部署中的 4 个 nginx pod 的服务。使用以下 YAML 清单：
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: Deployment
metadata:
  name: my-nginx-deploy
spec:
  replicas: 4
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: my-nginx
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: my-nginx
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: nginx-container
        image: nginx:latest
        ports:
        - containerPort: 80
```
