将给定的 DaemonSet YAML 中的主机目录 /var/log/nginx 挂载到 nginx 容器的 /var/log/nginx。
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: ds-hostpath
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: ds-hostpath
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: ds-hostpath
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: nginx
        image: nginx:latest
```
