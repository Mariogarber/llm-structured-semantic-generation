Create a RoleBinding named "my-role-binding" in the default namespace, binding user "my-user" to Role "my-role". Role "my-role" grants permission to "get" pods.
```
apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
kind: Role
metadata:
  name: my-role
  namespace: default
rules:
- apiGroups: [""]
  resources: ["pods"]
  verbs: ["get"]
```
