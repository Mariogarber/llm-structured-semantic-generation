在 DaemonSet YAML 中，将 `REGISTRY_HOST` 环境变量定义为 `kube-registry-modified.svc.cluster.local`，将 `REGISTRY_PORT` 定义为 `5000`。容器应该在容器端口 80 上暴露端口 `registry`，映射到主机端口 5000。
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: kube-registry-proxy-modified
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: kube-registry-modified
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: kube-registry-modified
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: kube-registry-proxy-modified
        image: nginx:latest
        resources:
          limits:
            cpu: 100m
            memory: 50Mi
```
