Add entries to hosts file: resolve foo.local, bar.local to 127.0.0.1 and foo.remote, bar.remote to 10.1.2.3 in the following YAML for a Pod:
```
apiVersion: v1
kind: Pod
metadata:
  name: hostaliases-pod
spec:
  containers:
  - name: cat-hosts
    image: busybox:1.28
    command:
    - cat
    args:
    - "/etc/hosts"
```
