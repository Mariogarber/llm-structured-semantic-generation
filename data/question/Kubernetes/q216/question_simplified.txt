Modify existing K8s ClusterRole YAML as follows: (a) Limit "services" verbs to "get"; (b) Remove "jobs" permissions in "batch" apiGroup; (c) Add "watch" permission for configmaps in "" apiGroup. Given:
```
apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
kind: ClusterRole
metadata:
  name: existing-clusterrole
rules:
  - apiGroups: [""]
    resources: ["pods", "services"]
    verbs: ["get", "list", "watch"]
  - apiGroups: ["apps"]
    resources: ["deployments"]
    verbs: ["get", "list"]
  - apiGroups: ["batch"]
    resources: ["jobs"]
    verbs: ["create", "delete"]
```
