Enhance DaemonSet to schedule pods only on nodes with label kubernetes.io/os: linux.
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: my-ds
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      name: my-ds
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        name: my-ds
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: nginx
        image: nginx
```
