Define `REGISTRY_HOST` env var as `kube-registry-modified.svc.cluster.local` and `REGISTRY_PORT` as `5000` in the DaemonSet YAML. Container should expose port `registry` on container port 80, mapping to host port 5000.
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: kube-registry-proxy-modified
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: kube-registry-modified
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: kube-registry-modified
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: kube-registry-proxy-modified
        image: nginx:latest
        resources:
          limits:
            cpu: 100m
            memory: 50Mi
```
