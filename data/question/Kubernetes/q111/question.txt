How can I configure a Kubernetes job manifest (YAML) such that every time I run the manifest a job will get created with kube-job- as a prefix? 
Label the job with "jobgroup: jexample" and use the following example as the spec.
```
spec:
  template:
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: my-container
        image: busybox
        command: ["echo", "Hello from the job"]
      restartPolicy: OnFailure
```
