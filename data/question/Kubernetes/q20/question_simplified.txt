Configure DNS for DaemonSet dnsconfig by adding nameserver "8.8.8.8". Given:
```
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: DaemonSet
metadata:
  name: dnsconfig
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: dnsconfig
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: dnsconfig
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: nginx
        image: nginx:latest
```
