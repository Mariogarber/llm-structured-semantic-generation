Create a RoleBinding named 'testadminbinding' in the 'test2' namespace, binding the 'testadmin' role to the 'myaccount' service account in the same namespace.
```
apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
kind: Role
metadata:
  namespace: test2
  name: testadmin
rules:
- apiGroups: ["*"]
  resources: ["*"]
  verbs: ["*"]
```
